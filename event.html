Volunteer Globally 2020 - Nepal

5 MAY 2019 AUTHOR: TAHA MURTAZA

Volunteer Globally 2020 - School building mission in Nepal

Volunteer Globally is one of the main pillars of Dubai Cares’ community outreach activities. It offers members of the UAE community a rare opportunity to join the Dubai Cares’ team in a remote and undeserved community to construct a school. The school-building mission will take place in Nepal from 15 to 23 March 2020.
