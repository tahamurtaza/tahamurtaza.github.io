Back to School Campaign for the Underprivileged Children of Kaolack Region

The program’s scale-up aims to support the rehabilitation of education services in Mosul and Baghdad following the large-scale displacement crisis that hit the country. The program’s activities include refurbishment of schools, building of community centers, improving early childhood care and development services, providing a safe and inclusive space for community led interventions and youth empowerment activities, as well as psychosocial support.
